### Controls
*   **Mouse**: Your character follows your cursor (with intentional driver lag).
*   **Keyboard**: Use `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` or `Arrow Keys` for manual override.
*   **Reboot**: Press `R` or click the button on the crash screen to restart.
